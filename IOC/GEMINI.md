# GEMINI.md

## Directory Overview

This directory contains the components of a research paper or project written in Czech. The project's focus is on analyzing and evaluating the capabilities of freely available large language models (LLMs). It includes the project's goals, an outline, the experimental design, an overview of the models being tested, and the methodology for evaluating the results.

## Key Files

*   **`AI a její schopnosti _ Cíl IOČ.txt`**: Outlines the main goal of the project: to analyze the abilities of free LLMs in areas like general knowledge, logic, math, and programming.
*   **`AI a její schopnosti _ Osnova_Plán IOČ.txt`**: Provides the structure and plan for the research paper, from introduction to conclusion.
*   **`Navrh_Experimentu.md`**: Details the specific experiments to be conducted. This includes the questions, problems, and tasks that will be given to the LLMs, as well as the criteria for evaluation.
*   **`Prehled_modelu.md`**: Contains a review of the free LLMs that are the subject of the study, including Gemini, Claude, Llama, and others.
*   **`Vyhodnoceni_Vysledku.md`**: Describes how the results of the experiments will be collected, analyzed, and interpreted, using both quantitative and qualitative methods.

## Usage

The files in this directory are the source materials for a research paper. They can be used to understand the project's methodology, the specific tests being performed, and the planned analysis. The final paper would be synthesized from these documents.
